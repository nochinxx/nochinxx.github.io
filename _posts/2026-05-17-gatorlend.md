<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4 border-b-2 border-indigo-200 pb-2"> Overview </h2>

GatorLend is a platform for structuring ownership and transfer of real-world assets, starting with campus items such as textbooks, calculators, and lab equipment. The system represents those assets on XRPL and treats exchange as a transparent record-keeping and coordination problem.

The project is less about building a simple marketplace and more about defining how ownership, transfer, and verification should work when assets move across users and systems.

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-8 mb-4 border-b-2 border-indigo-200 pb-2"> Direction </h2>

GatorLend is an example of starting with a narrow use case while thinking toward larger financial infrastructure questions: transparent ownership systems, accountable records, and coordination mechanisms that can eventually map onto real institutional workflows.
